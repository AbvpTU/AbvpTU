Know Your Roots

One day, two reasons to celebrate student power.

Every 9th of July, ABVP workers across the country come together to mark National Students Day and the founding of the Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad — a coincidence of dates that reminds every student of the ideals they carry forward.

01 9 July · National

National Students Day

Observed on the birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda, National Students Day celebrates his message of self-belief, character-building and service to the motherland — values that continue to guide student movements across India.

02 1949 · Origin

History of ABVP

Founded on 9 July 1949 by Prof. Yashwantrao Kelkar, the Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad grew from a small student circle into the world's largest student organisation, working on campuses for academics, culture and nation-building.

03 Tripura University

ABVP at TU

The Tripura University unit carries this legacy forward on campus — leading student welfare drives, cultural programmes, academic support cells and social outreach, while training a new generation of campus leaders.
